Welcome to Inshrah Writes

Assalam o alaikum! I’m Inshrah Nawaz. A passionate writer! I started writing 4 years back in my matric. The reason of continuing this writing were my friends, my teachers, my support system. They never let me quit. I never believed I’m good at writing, but they convinced me. I started writing for my people. Since, I’m a patriot, the conditions of my country and the society used to hurt me alot. So, I tried to change the society by my words. Then, I started writing social issues, fiction, fantasy. Later on, I wrote about pain, hope, optimism, heart breaks and islamic topics too. I pen down everything that I wanted my readers to know. I’m a learner and a teacher at the same time. I believe ” Readers make a writer.” and in my case it’s so relatable. The people who read my content, my write ups, who appreciated me, made me a writer. I write for them. I write to fill their hollowness and emptiness. I love when people find my posts relatable. It takes blood and sweat to produce good content. It takes courage to write what you feel. That’s why not everyone is a writer. It takes life experiences to pen down what you gained and what you lost. So, yeah I write with my full heart. Everything takes hardwork. People think I’ve been taking classes for writing or I’ve learned it from somewhere. Nah! Never did I do that. Emotions flow through my pen automatically. /
